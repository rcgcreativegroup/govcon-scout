## Underpricing Risks

- **Callbacks not priced in.** FFP means re-treatment is at no additional cost to the Government. Each CLIN unit price must absorb callback frequency for that service type.
- **Access / scheduling overhead omitted.** Base entry delays, escort time, and school-day coordination add real time cost. Field-only quotes from subs may not capture this.
- **Material escalation over option years.** Pesticides, supplies, and equipment costs can rise over a 5-year contract. Fixed unit prices may compress margin in later option years.
- **Emergency / after-hours scope unclear.** Confirm whether the PWS requires out-of-hours response and whether it is included in base CLINs or separately priced.
- **Sub quote obtained too late.** If subcontractor pricing is gathered informally or at the last minute, unit prices may not reflect actual execution cost. Get quotes early.

## Overpricing Risks

- **Active small business competition.** Multiple established vendors have won similar federal pest control awards. Pricing well above the market median risks non-competitive standing.
